        How to fight

        Left side: drag to move · flick up to jump · flick down to fast-fall / drop through

        Right side: tap for a quick attack aimed where you're moving · swipe for a strong attack in that direction (hold to charge it) — swipe up in the air to rise, down to spike!

        Weapons: your strong attack is your equipped weapon — bare fists smash, the sword lunge-slashes for big damage, magic fires knockback bursts that fly further the longer you charge (watch your mana!)

        Dash attack: tap while at full run to slide in swinging — big launch, but miss and you skid past wide open!

        Duck & roll: hold down to guard · flick sideways while ducked to dodge-roll through attacks (costs guard)

        Quick fall: tap down mid-jump to cut it short — keep drifting as you drop and you'll land in a slick slide you can attack from!

        Corner buttons: your two abilities

        Controller: stick moves (hold down to duck) · A jump · X quick attack · B/Y smash · bumpers/triggers = abilities

        Rack up their % — the higher it is, the further they fly. Knock them off to score!
